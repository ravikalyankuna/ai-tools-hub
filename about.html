How AI Tools Hub works

AI Tools Hub is a discovery website that helps visitors find, compare, and understand AI products across writing, coding, design, video, research, support, and productivity workflows. The site is designed to make large tool lists easier to browse and to provide a guided starting point for beginners.

Editorial approach

We review official product pages, public feature documentation, and practical workflow fit before assigning each tool to a category. Descriptions, features, and use cases are presented in structured format so readers can compare tools faster.

Ratings and recommendations

Ratings on this site are editorial summaries intended for quick discovery. They are not guarantees, certifications, legal opinions, or endorsements. Visitors should verify pricing, compliance, and product suitability on each vendor's official website before making a decision.

External links

Tool buttons on this site lead to the official websites of third-party products. We do not control those external sites and their content or policies may change over time.

Site quality commitment

We aim to keep navigation clear, publish original explanatory content, and maintain visible policy pages so users can understand how the site operates.
