What “free” means here

Some tools are fully free and some are freemium. In both cases, the tool can typically be tested without paying upfront, although limits and plan rules can change.

How to use this page

Start by category, shortlist only similar tools, then validate them on your own files, prompts, or workflow instead of relying only on landing-page claims.

What to compare

Compare limits, watermarking, export quality, collaboration restrictions, and how quickly the tool pushes you into a paid upgrade.
